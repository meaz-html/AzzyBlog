Hello again, first of all, sorry for not posting that much, I had exams and was busy a lot of the time too. I am currently in the process of moving into a Astro powered blog (well, im writing in it cant you see?).

Anyways, So you all know that flirb died permanently in late February, and then this month I stumbled across a twitter-like revival called "Blueblips", It was made after the flirb shutdown, I was really obsessed with it, heck I even moderate the discord server now.

Its kinda new, It came out to the public last month and the main website is still in construction.

The API is the main part, you can patch official twitter clients (except for android, but you can patch third parties) and they work, similar to what flirb did.

Of course, the big difference is that flirb was not "old twitter revival" themed, it was more like "alternative social media", while Blueblips is heavily old twitter themed, they are also in the works of 2016/2017 themed twitter from what I heard.
